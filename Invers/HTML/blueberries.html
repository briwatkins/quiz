Blueberries


Blueberries | A morning day and a garden play,
I'm already dirty with blueberry paint.
My thoughts allude to the grace of Jesus who has washed me clean with His blood.
A white canvas I became, a painter He is.
He showed me a world full of His eternal gifts.
He painted my story to reflect His beautiful glory,
and He continues to transform me daily.
He colored me crazy with His crazy love,
I overflow with joy from the Good Man above.
