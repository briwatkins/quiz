Belonging


Belonging | The wind howls as it calls my name,
speaking louder and louder until I notice.
The water glistens to show me something.
But what? My ears are too deaf,
my eyes are too blind,
and my heart is too broken to understand the language of nature.
But as the flowers sprout up and the trees praise the sky,
I start to realize the family I belong in.
